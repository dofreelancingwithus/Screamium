Our Story

From a small dream to a delicious reality, here's how Screamium Ice Cream became the name on everyone's lips.

Screamium Ice Cream started with one simple idea: to create ice cream that doesn’t just taste amazing but also tells a story. Founded by Abhinav Padige and his team, Screamium combines innovation and passion to craft flavors that ignite the imagination and delight the palate.

From the first scoop, we’ve been committed to using only the finest ingredients, paired with sustainable practices and a touch of magic. Our journey has been about creating an unforgettable experience for every ice cream lover out there.

Our Mission

At Screamium, our mission is simple yet powerful: to spread happiness, one scoop at a time. We believe in the power of ice cream to bring people together, spark joy, and create cherished memories.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in the ice cream industry by constantly innovating and setting new standards for quality, flavor, and sustainability. We aim to redefine the way the world experiences ice cream.

Our Values

  • Quality First: Every batch of Screamium is crafted with care and precision.
  • Customer Delight: We put our customers at the heart of everything we do.
  • Innovation: Pioneering unique flavors and concepts that surprise and inspire.
  • Sustainability: Protecting our planet for future generations.
